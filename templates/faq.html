What is the weather like in December?

Hot with a good chance of rain.
Highs of 88°F / 31°C
Lows of 73°F / 23°C

Aka, its pretty darn hot in December, so channel your inner Bawa by bringing airy, tropics-friendly clothes (think: swim suits, sandals, breathable materials).

Where can I find Indian clothes?

Beg, borrow, steal! If those don't work, here are a couple online options:

indianweddingsaree.com - Sells more than just saris and they have readymade sizes you can choose from. But, buyer beware, if the item is surprisingly inexpensive, it will not look as nice as it does in photos.

sareesaga.com - They do custom sizing and other customizations. Heard good things about this site.

Other options: cbazaar.com, kalkifashion.com
